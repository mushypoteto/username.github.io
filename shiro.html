Xiaomi 17 Pro Series: Redefining the Next Generation of Premium Smartphones

Xiaomi 17 Pro series, comprising Xiaomi 17 Pro and Xiaomi 17 Pro Max, embody Xiaomi's most revolutionary innovations, featuring a seamless symphony of dual displays and intelligent connection, powered by Snapdragon® 8 Elite Gen 5 Mobile Platform and an industry-leading powerhouse endurance.

Xiaomi 17 Pro series enjoy a sleek 8mm-thick body design, and available in four colors — Black, White, Purple, and Green.

Groundbreaking Dual-Screen Design Revolutionizes Mobile Interactions

Xiaomi 17 Pro introduce a revolutionary dual-screen design that blends sophisticated aesthetics with intuitive, intelligent functionality. The Dynamic Back Display enhances lifestyle and efficiency under various scenarios: customizable watch faces, AI wallpapers, virtual pets, dynamic information notifications, sticky notes, and so much more.

Meanwhile, the Dynamic Back Display provides a wide range of real-time notification, such as flight notifications, train ticket updates, takeaway delivery or ride-hailing status, music playback... the list goes on. Moreover, the back display supports both preview and selfie functions, allowing users to shoot selfies directly using the main cameras. Close the main displays, this device will instantly transform into Handheld-Console Mode. It fits comfortably in one hand without missing any notifications shown on the back, and with the exclusive case connected via Bluetooth, it turns into a retro gaming console.

Innovative Breakthrough in OLED Screen Technology

Xiaomi 17 Pro series features the innovative M10 Display Panel and SuperRED luminous material, achieving excellent efficiency with ultra-high brightness and ultra-low power consumption. Additionally, Xiaomi 17 Pro Max exclusively adopts the independent pixel array technology, achieving sharper clarity while consuming less power than conventional 2K displays. Xiaomi 17 Pro Max is also equipped with Xiaomi Shield Glass 3.0, a super solid display glass to secure safe, sturdy use of the screen.

Leica Summilux and LOFIC Technology to Achieve Pro-level Cinematic Imaging

Xiaomi 17 Pro series features the Leica Summilux imaging system, meeting full creative camera needs. The main camera meets all-round optical capability upgrades, with an f/1.67 large aperture, a 1G+6P lens, and anti-glare lens coating, powered by Light Fusion 950L with a large 1/1.28-inch size sensor and next-generation LOFIC High Dynamic technology, achieving an ultra-high 16.5EV sensor dynamic range. Xiaomi 17 Pro debuts an inverted floating telephoto lens and supports 20cm telephoto macro shots. Xiaomi 17 Pro Max features a powerful 5x periscope telephoto lens with an expanded 1/2-inch sensor, an f/2.6 large aperture, and hardware-level HDR support. Its new light-gathering prism structure significantly enhances light capture, resulting in a 30% increase in light intake.

Slim Design and Long Battery Life Showcasing Battery Tech Prowess

Incorporating Xiaomi Surge Battery, Xiaomi 17 Pro series features a high 16% silicon content for high energy density, and L-shaped stacked battery maximizing internal space utilization to significantly boost energy density while maintaining a slim form factor. The breakthrough results in a 6300mAh battery for Xiaomi 17 Pro, while Xiaomi 17 Pro Max reaches 7500mAh. Xiaomi 17 Pro series models support 100W wired charging, 50W wireless charging, and the 100W PPS universal fast charging protocol, enabling convenient charging experience. Furthermore, with UWB-enabled smart car key functionality for keyless access and barrier-free interactions, the phone series redefines intelligent and integrated digital living with a previously unseen level of smart connectivity.
